ABOUT

Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology (SVNIT) is a public technical university located in Surat, Gujarat, India. It was established in 1961 as one of the Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs) and was later declared a "Deemed University" under the University Grants Commission Act, of 1956. In 2007, it was granted the status of an "Institute of National Importance." SVNIT offers undergraduate courses in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer, Production, and Chemical Engineering undergraduate programs. It also provides postgraduate programs, M.Sc. programs, and a doctoral program in various branches of engineering.

The American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE) serves as the Global Home of Chemical Engineers. It is the world's leading professional organization for students and professionals in Chemical Engineering. Established in 1910, and with currently over 60,000 members from 110 countries, AIChE Global provides a worldwide platform for aspiring minds to showcase their talents, and equips them with a vast wealth of resources and guidance, enabling the talented individuals to achieve success in their future endeavors. AIChE has the breadth of resources and expertise you need whether you are in core process industries or emerging areas, such as translational medicine.

The Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) at SVNIT was established on January 16, 2014, and is affiliated with the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE). ChES aims to disseminate knowledge and practical experience in chemical engineering education and its applications among future chemical engineers. It provides a platform for the SVNIT chemical engineering community to engage in discussions, collaborative problem-solving, and skill development. ChES organizes events, seminars, and workshops that focus on enhancing public understanding of technical issues and nurturing individual curiosity within the field of chemical engineering.

The Student Regional Conference (SRC) is an annual three-day event organized by AIChE Global in each of the fourteen regions based on geographical locations. The conference brings together various AIChE chapters to collaborate on shared interests through training workshops, competitive events, and insightful seminars. It is a platform for budding Chemical Engineers to interact with peers and industry experts, fostering essential workplace skills. SRC allows students to showcase their talents and knowledge in various topics related to Chemical Engineering, with winners competing at the Annual Student Conference (ASC) held in the USA.

Chem-E-Car

The Chem-E-Car competition, organized by the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), challenges students majoring in Chemical Engineering to design and construct small-scale automobiles powered by chemical energy sources. These cars are built to run a specific distance and stop safely. Participants must adhere to operating their cars using "green" methods, ensuring no visible pollution or waste in the form of liquid or gas is released, such as exhaust. Participants are evaluated on the basis of poster design, drive system creativity, vehicle design, performance, safety application, consistency, and team spirit.
